FAQ
Common Questions
Is the HWID fingerprinting bypass-proof?
We use multi-vector hardware analysis including disk IDs, motherboard serials, and network adapters. While no system is 100% bypass-proof, NyxAuth is one of the most resilient layers available.
Can I self-host my Discord bot?
Yes. Business Onyx users can provide their own bot tokens to host private bot instances linked to the NyxAuth engine on your own server.
Is the free tier really free forever?
Yes. The Phantom tier is free with no time limit. You can build and deploy real projects on it. Upgrade only when you need more capacity.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your billing portal. No hidden fees, no cancellation charges. Your account reverts to the free Phantom tier.
