    Is the HWID fingerprinting bypass-proof?
    We use multi-vector hardware analysis including disk IDs, motherboard serials, and network adaptations. While no system is 100% bypass-proof, ShadowAuth is one of the most resilient layers in the market.
    Can I self-host the bots?
    Yes. Business Onyx users can provide their own API keys for Discord and Telegram to host their own private bot instances linked to our engine.
    Do you support custom domains?
    Custom domains for dashboard and API endpoints are available for Business Onyx subscribers.
